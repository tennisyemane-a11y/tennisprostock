The Signature

The gold serve-arc — the trajectory of a struck ball — is the brand's recurring mark. It appears as the logo's flourish, the hero's animated line, and the section dividers. It encodes the "explosive jump serve" without ever being literal. Use sparingly; it should feel like a signature, not a pattern.
