The Coach

Yemane Tekeste

A modern performance coach delivering elite development through a private-club experience.

Trained in the systems that shape today's tour-level athletes, Yemane built his coaching practice around a single idea: that serious players deserve coaching that is as refined as it is relentless. Every session blends explosive, results-driven athleticism with the quiet discipline of a private club.

The philosophy is simple — measure everything, waste nothing. From footwork mechanics to match psychology, development is structured, tracked, and personal. No two players follow the same path, and no detail is left to chance.
