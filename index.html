The Coach

Tennis is not taught.
It is uncovered.

I'm Yemane Tekeste. For over a decade I've lived inside this game — as a competitor, a student of its history, and now as a coach to players who want more than rallies: they want transformation.

My philosophy is simple. Technique is the entrance, but mindset is the mansion. Every session is built around three pillars — precision, composure, and quiet confidence — until your game feels less like effort and more like signature.

Students stay because progress here is measured, personal, and impossible to ignore.
